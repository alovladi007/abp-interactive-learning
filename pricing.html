Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the remaining balance will be credited to your account.

Is there a free trial for Pro and ProMax plans?

Yes! Both Pro and ProMax plans come with a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with our service, contact our support team for a full refund.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students with a valid .edu email address can get 50% off on Pro and ProMax plans. Contact our support team with your student ID for verification.
