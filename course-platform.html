Lesson Summary

This introductory lesson provides a comprehensive overview of the SAT exam, including its structure, scoring system, and importance in college admissions. You'll learn about the different sections of the test, the types of questions you'll encounter, and fundamental strategies for success.

We'll cover the recent changes to the SAT format, including the shift to digital testing, and how these changes affect your preparation strategy. By the end of this lesson, you'll have a clear understanding of what to expect on test day.
