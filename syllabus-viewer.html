    Course Policies

    Attendance Policy

    Regular attendance is essential for success in this course. Students are expected to attend all scheduled class sessions. More than 3 unexcused absences may result in grade reduction.

    Late Work Policy

    Late assignments will be accepted with a 10% penalty per day, up to 3 days late. After 3 days, assignments will not be accepted unless prior arrangements have been made.

    Academic Integrity

    All work submitted must be original. Plagiarism or cheating will result in a failing grade for the assignment and may lead to course failure. Please refer to the university's academic integrity policy.

    Accommodations

    Students with documented disabilities who may need accommodations should contact the Office of Disability Services and make an appointment with the instructor as soon as possible.
